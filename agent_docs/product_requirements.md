# Product Requirements (MVP Scope)

## Primary User Story
The user decides they want to take a weekend trip. They open the app, enter their destination, dates, budget, and vibes. Instantly, they receive a fully realistic, day-by-day itinerary with an interactive map and direct ways to book flights and hotels. They can customize the schedule via drag-and-drop, and the app optimizes the route.

## Must-Have Features
- **Smart Onboarding Prompt:** Clean intake form for trip parameters.
- **Two-Brain Generation:** Gemini generates the text plan; Google Places API validates locations and fetches coordinates.
- **Integrated Bookings Hub:** Direct links to book flights, hotels, and rentals based on budget.
- **Interactive Timeline & Map UI:** List/calendar views paired with a Mapbox map highlighting attractions.
- **Customization Engine:** Drag-and-drop itinerary editing and route optimization.

## Excluded from MVP
- Social Collaboration / Inviting Friends.

## Success Metrics
- Downloads & Subscriptions (Month 1 target validation).
- Profit Margin (Break-even on API costs by Month 3).
- User Retention and App Store Feedback.
